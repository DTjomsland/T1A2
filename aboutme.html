Let's talk about me.

Hello, my name is David Tjomsland. Originally from California, I now live in beautiful Perth, Australia. I have a passion for teaching and creating, both of which I thoroughly love directing toward web development. From game designer to computer scientist, I spent my childhood dreaming about being a part of the industry in any way possible. As long as I had a computer in my hands I knew I would be happy. So, what did I do when I graduated high school? I went out and got myself a degree in history, of course. After years of being a high school teacher, teaching various subjects ranging from woodshop to physics, I find myself here in front of a computer, on the opposite side of the world, living the life teenage me always dreamt of. I couldn't be any happier.
